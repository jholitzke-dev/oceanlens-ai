What OceanLens can measure

Surface buoy

Water temperature, waves, wind, pressure, air temperature, and visibility where reported.

Cannot provide a water-column depth profile.

Underwater glider

Depth-resolved temperature, salinity, oxygen, chlorophyll, pressure, and currents where equipped.

Profiles are mobile snapshots, not permanent stations.

Optical satellite

Surface color, chlorophyll, turbidity, and possible shallow-water depth under clear conditions.

Limited by clouds, haze, glint, and water clarity.

SAR satellite

Cloud-independent surface roughness, storms, wave/wind signatures, and coastlines.

Cannot directly measure underwater chemistry or depth.
